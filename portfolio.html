Vidego offers users the ability to perform text searches on the content of video files. We are able to direct you to the portion of the video relevant to your query, by conducting speech recognition searches and nautral language processing.



Global EngAge @ HackFest - 1st Place Winner
Undergrduate team lead; Product Management

Global EngAGE is a multidisciplinary, international team of students in computer science, business administration and healthcare. The team won the 1st prize at the LeadingAge Hackfest 2013 with its healthcare application "EngAGE."

EngAGE enables homebound elders to remotely participate in activities that are organized by retirement communities or senior centers. The application populates a personalized list of events through processing of natural language; when the user selects an ongoing event, it loads an easy-to-use video conferencing application, where they can not only see what is going on but also directly interact with the other people engaging in the same event.
